About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We combine academic insight through UK university partnerships with real-world projects to prepare practitioners for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Model

Academy

Bootcamps and training for students and professionals.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing IP, future risk models, and AI governance.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Community

We serve students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure, helping them upskill in digital and cyber risk.
